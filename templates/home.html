Attendace Poller









Overview

This project mainly concentrate in attendance marking in online class.It gives a solution to Teachers who are uncertain about student activities during online class. Here students are monitored throughout the class each frame of a student is taken and checked whether it could find a face or not and if it finds a face it is compared with the available encodings to detect the face.This monitoring takes place throughout the class and starting time and ending time are marked. And the time interval is calculated between each starting and ending time ,finally at the end of the class percentage is calculated . This percentage says how long they are present in front of the camera.


Challenges

To maintain the attendance record with day to day activities is a challenging task. The conventional method of calling name of each student even in online classess is time consuming and there is always a chance of proxy attendance. And also in current system which introduced automatic attendance marking by face detection and recognition ,here the students attendance is marked once they just login to the channel /meeting . This overcomes the conventional method of calling out name but does not ensures the attention of student . The draw back over this is even when the students are not present lively in class or they sleep or do suspicious activities rather listening class, the attendance is just marked as present because they have logged into the meet. So in our project we have challenges to overcome the traditional method of attendance system and also to ensure whether students pay attention throughout the class and are present in the class lively.


Problem Statement

In most of the referred papers they have proposed works for offline attendance marking system and biometric-enabled system. In current system full time (ie,throughout the class)live capturing of video is not done which results in lack of attention of students. We have used algorithms to capture the video throughout the class as attendance will be marked based on the live capturing in which when students are out of the frame it is marked as end-time which reduces the attendance percentage of that particular student so there are chances that students pay attention and stay in the frame throughout the class to maintain their attendance percentage. And this will lead to genuine attention of students throughout the class.
